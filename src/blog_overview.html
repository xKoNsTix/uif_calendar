Fago After Dark

Introducing "Fago After Dark" - a new opportunity for campus engagement! Imagine a space where students can come together to collaborate and engage in intercurricular activities, like the Get2 Initiative. That's what "Fago After Dark" is all about. Once every two weeks, the campus cafe, Fago, will open its doors for these activities. Whether you're looking to start a club, organize a community event, or simply connect with other students, "Fago After Dark" offers the perfect space to make it happen. Don't miss out on this exciting opportunity to enhance your college experience. Be a part of the "Fago After Dark" movement and take your involvement on campus to the next level!
